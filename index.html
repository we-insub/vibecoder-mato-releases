Mato Helper

Blog Automation Tool for Google Blogger

About This Application

Mato Helper is a desktop application that helps users create, manage, and publish blog posts to Google Blogger (Blogspot). It streamlines the content creation workflow by automating repetitive tasks such as text generation, image handling, and post publishing.

What Does Mato Helper Do?

  • Blog Post Creation — Generate and format blog posts with AI assistance, then publish directly to your Google Blogger account.
  • Image Management — Upload images to Google Drive for use in blog posts, replacing embedded images with hosted URLs for better performance.
  • Bulk Publishing — Create and publish multiple blog posts efficiently with batch processing.
  • Draft Management — Save posts as drafts on Blogger for review before publishing.

Google Account Usage

Mato Helper uses Google OAuth 2.0 to securely access your Google account. Specifically, it requests access to:

  • Google Blogger API — To create, edit, and publish blog posts on your Blogger account.
  • Google Drive API (drive.file scope) — To upload images that are used in your blog posts. Only files created by this app are accessible; your other Drive files are not accessed.

Your Google credentials are never stored on our servers. Authentication is handled entirely through Google's secure OAuth 2.0 flow.

Data Privacy

  • We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.
  • All blog content is created locally on your device and published directly to your Google Blogger account.
  • Images are uploaded to your own Google Drive account.
  • No data is transmitted to third-party servers other than Google's APIs.
